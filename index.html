What is a GPT?

A custom AI tool that can perform specific tasks like writing content, answering questions, generating ideas, or solving problems.

A GPT (short for Generative Pre-trained Transformer) is a custom AI tool built inside ChatGPT that can perform specific tasks like writing content, answering questions, generating ideas, or solving problems, based on how it's trained and prompted.

Think of it like a mini app built on top of AI, a specialized tool rather than a general chatbot.

What Are the Benefits of Using a GPT?

Whether someone is a business owner, creator, coach, or just needs help with everyday tasks. GPTs make life easier, faster, and more productive.

  • Saves Time: GPTs can write content, answer questions, and complete tasks in seconds, so users don't have to start from scratch.
  • Boosts Productivity: They can help with writing emails, blog posts, social media captions, product ideas, and more, quickly and accurately.
  • Easy to Use: Anyone can chat with a GPT like they're messaging a friend. No learning curve, no special skills needed.
  • Customized: Each GPT is built to do something specific, like help with product creation, business ideas, content writing, or affiliate marketing.
  • Always Available: A GPT doesn't sleep or take breaks. It's ready to help 24/7, whenever the user needs it.
  • Affordable Help: It's like having a virtual assistant, but without paying a full-time salary.
